What is Junk To Your Trunk?

The aim of this website is to promote the reduction of waste, specifically from commercial and building sites.
Many people are finding ways to repurpose waste materials, such as brick or wood.
Use this site to list your waste materials, and if someone in the local area has a use for them, then they will purchase your listing, and come and take it off your hands.

As a buyer, you might be in search for these waste materials for a use on your own projects. Search by location to see if there are any listings near you.

All profits will be donated to charities promoting corporate sustainability.
